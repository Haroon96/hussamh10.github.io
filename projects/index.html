To Act or React: Investigating Proactive Strategies For Online Community Moderation

 

Hussam Habib, Maaz Bin Musa, Fareed Zaffar, Rishab Nithyanand

ICWSM '21

 

We test the feasibility of proactive moderation of dangerous communities. We study the topical and participant evolution of online communities on Reddit and exploit the unique evolutionary pattern of dangerous communities to flag evolution of benign communities towards dangerous communties

Hussam Habib, Rishab Nithyanand

Submitted

 

We test whether Reddit's community level interventions are driven by media and internal pressure. Furthermore, we test whether media driven interventions are effective in curbing problematic behavior.
